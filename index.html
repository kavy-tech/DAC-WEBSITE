About Data Analytics Club

The Data Analytics Club (DAC) is a student-led organization at Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College, University of Delhi, dedicated to building a strong foundation in data analytics, artificial intelligence, and problem-solving through experiential learning.

At DAC, we bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world application. By combining structured learning with hands-on projects, workshops, and industry exposure, we provide students across all experience levels with a clear pathway to develop practical, job-ready skills.

Members gain exposure to industry-relevant tools such as Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, and AI/ML techniques, while strengthening their analytical thinking, communication, and decision-making abilities. Through collaborative learning, case-based problem-solving, and guided mentorship, students learn not just how to analyze data but how to derive meaningful insights.

More than a club, DAC is a growth-driven community that fosters curiosity, consistency, and collaboration. We empower students to build confidence, showcase their work, and prepare for impactful careers in analytics, consulting, data science, and beyond.

Initiatives & Programs

Analytics Bootcamp

Intensive, instructor-led crash courses focused on Python for data, SQL, statistics for modelling, and end-to-end ML pipelines. Each bootcamp ends with capstone mini-projects judged by faculty and alumni.

Project Labs

Semester-long, mentor-backed projects where small teams collaborate with NGOs, campus departments, or local businesses to deliver dashboards, forecasts, and insights that solve real problems.

Skill Tracks & Certification

Curated learning tracks (Data Engineering, Machine Learning, Visualization, Analytics Consulting) with micro-certificates on completion. Tracks combine recorded modules, weekly labs, and assessment challenges.

Case Competitions & Hackathons

With a community of 500+ students, DAC actively engages members through hands-on projects, hackathons, workshops, case studies, and collaborative learning experiences.

Research Cell

A small group that publishes exploratory reports, datasets, and reproducible notebooks — useful for students preparing academic posters, internships, or research assistantships.

Industry Connect & Mentorship

Through continuous learning, peer collaboration, and exposure to industry-relevant practices, DAC creates an environment where curiosity turns into capability and interest into impact.
