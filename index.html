About Data Analytics Club — DDUC

01.

Who We Are

Data Analytics Club (DAC) at DDU College is a student-run learning community that equips learners with practical data skills, domain insight, and industry-ready project experience. We bring together beginners and advanced practitioners to work on real datasets, build decision-ready models, and run applied analytics workshops and competitions.

02.

Our Approach

Our approach is hands-on: short instructor-led modules, multi-week project tracks, and mentorship from alumni and industry volunteers. We serve as a bridge between classroom theory and workplace expectations — preparing students for internships, research, and analytics roles.

Initiatives & Programs

Analytics Bootcamp

Intensive, instructor-led crash courses focused on Python for data, SQL, statistics for modelling, and end-to-end ML pipelines. Each bootcamp ends with capstone mini-projects judged by faculty and alumni.

Project Labs

Semester-long, mentor-backed projects where small teams collaborate with NGOs, campus departments, or local businesses to deliver dashboards, forecasts, and insights that solve real problems.

Skill Tracks & Certification

Curated learning tracks (Data Engineering, Machine Learning, Visualization, Analytics Consulting) with micro-certificates on completion. Tracks combine recorded modules, weekly labs, and assessment challenges.

Case Competitions & Hackathons

Domain-specific competitions that simulate consulting and analytics problem solving under time pressure. Teams present evidence-backed recommendations and deploy lightweight prototypes.

Research Cell

A small group that publishes exploratory reports, datasets, and reproducible notebooks — useful for students preparing academic posters, internships, or research assistantships.

Industry Connect & Mentorship

Regular office hours and project reviews with alumni and industry volunteers. We help with resume reviews, case-interview prep, and internship referrals where possible.
